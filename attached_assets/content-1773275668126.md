### Unique Flavours

We pride ourselves on churning unique flavours that may sound crazy. Enjoy seasonally rotating flavours like Sauerkraut, Summer Corn, Sweet Potato Casserole, Mango Habanero, and many more.

![](https://urbanchurn.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/globe.png)

### Culturally Inspired

We churn ice cream and offer desserts inspired by cultures from around the world. Enjoy rotating desserts such as lemon bar sundaes, affogatos, tiramisu sundaes, dulce de leche and more.

![](https://urbanchurn.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/07/growth.png)

### Natural Ingredients

Enjoy your ice cream knowing there's NO artificial flavouring or food colouring like Yellow No 6. or Red 40. We use local PA 16% butterfat dairy and natural ingredients that are prepared in house.

## How Do Pre-Orders Work?

#### Choose Your Flavour(s)

Pre-order limited edition flavours that are updated weekly.

#### Determine Where to Pick Up

Determine which of our 3 shops you want to pick your order(s) up.

#### Remember To Pick Up

Add the pre-order date to your calendar to remember pickup.

#### Pre-Order Terms

We hold orders up to 2 weeks. If the item is not picked up on the 3rd week you may request a credit. On/after the 4th week the item is unfortunately no longer available and is nonrefundable.
